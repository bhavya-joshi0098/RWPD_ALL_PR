Student Co-Curricular Activities

Sports Competition

Students participate in inter-school sports competitions, showcasing their skills in basketball, football, and track events.

Science Fair

Innovative science projects displayed by students during the annual science fair, encouraging creativity and innovation.

Art Exhibition

An art exhibition where students display their artistic talents through paintings, sculptures, and craftwork.

Music Club

The music club allows students to practice and perform a variety of musical genres, from classical to contemporary.

Drama and Theater

Students get a chance to showcase their acting and directing skills through annual theater productions.

Debate Competition

Students actively participate in debate competitions, enhancing their communication and critical thinking skills.
